• WELCOME TO GHOOMAR VILLAGE •

Ghoomar Village is a 25,000 sq. ft. indoor celebration space designed as a living Rajasthani village. One ticket unlocks unlimited food, live folk performances, carnival games and cultural activities — all in one place, fully air-conditioned, with free basement parking.

Specially designed for large families, group gatherings and celebrations, it is Gurugram's blockbuster family entertainment destination — a space where every corner echoes with culture and every smile speaks of warmth.

25,000 sq. ft. festive setup 20+ starters unlimited 21+ main course items Fully Air Conditioned Free Basement Parking

• WHAT YOUR ENTRY INCLUDES •

Unlimited Food

20+ starters and 21+ main course items — 100% pure vegetarian, served Rajasthani style.

Kalbeliya Dancers

Live folk performances, Bhopa live music and the rhythm of Rajasthan all evening long.

Kathputli & Jaadugar

Puppet shows, magic acts and bioscope storytelling the whole family will remember.

Jyotish & Mehendi

Jyotish readings by Tota Pandit, Khel Gully games, mehendi art and more live experiences.

Champi Maalish

Relax with a traditional Rajasthani head massage right inside the village.

Pottery Making

Get your hands dirty and shape clay with a real Kumhar at our live pottery station.

Balloon Shooting & Darts

Classic carnival games for kids and adults — play, win, celebrate.

Apno Ka Saath

A group-friendly format built for large families, reunions, and celebrations.
