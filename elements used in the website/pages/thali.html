• ABOUT US •

Ghoomar Traditional Thali Restaurant is a premier and authentic Indian restaurant that has been proudly serving guests across India since 2016. Our mission has always been to combine a warm, enjoyable ambience with skillful cooking into one extraordinary dining experience.

We serve 100% pure vegetarian Rajasthani cuisine in the form of an unlimited serving thali. Ghoomar is synonymous with Rajasthani traditional thali food — a culinary journey rooted in heritage and royal hospitality.

• OUR MENU •

The chef-driven menu highlights seasonal ingredients in simple, handcrafted dishes served straight from our kitchen. Add even more to your experience with a selection from our complimentary beverages, and trust us — save room for dessert.
