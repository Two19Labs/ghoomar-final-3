Our Outlets

From Old Delhi to the Northeast,
Rajasthan follows you.

Three cities. Three outlets. One soul — the flavours, the music and the warmth of a Rajasthani haveli, no matter where you are.

The Setting

A haveli, inside the city.

Jharokha windows, folk motifs, earthy terracotta tones. Every corner of a Ghoomar outlet is designed to feel like you have walked into a Rajasthani home — the kind where the table is always set and the food never runs out.

dishes. One thali. Unlimited Rajasthan.

The Format

Unlimited. For real.

This is not a fixed plate. The moment something on your thali runs low, it is refilled — no questions, no extra charge. Our servers weave through the hall carrying fresh batches from the kitchen all evening.

The Premium Thali adds five richer preparations on top. Either way, you will not leave hungry.

The Dishes

Every dish made from scratch,
every single time.

No shortcuts. No pre-mixes. The dal is simmered, the bati is baked over coals, the jalebi is fried to order. Here is a taste of what arrives at your table.

The Experience

The Bhopa. The Pagdi.
The part that words struggle with.

A Bhopa musician plays live at your table — the ancient string of the Rajasthani jantar filling the room. At the end of your meal, you are dressed in a pagdi and a traditional dupatta for a photo you will actually keep.

These are not ticketed add-ons. They come with the thali. Visual documentation of this is on its way — until then, come see it for yourself.
